School Reopening Committee

Purpose of the Committee

Our School Reopening Committee was a group of educators, union representatives, and community members tasked with reviewing the Reopening Washington Schools 2020 - District Planning Guide from OSPI and working to formulate a school reopening plan specific to Monroe School District that best met the needs of our community while ensuring that mandatory health and safety requirements were met. The committee worked throughout June and July 2020 to make a recommendation to the school board, who was ultimately responsible for adopting a fall 2020 reopening plan through a board resolution.
